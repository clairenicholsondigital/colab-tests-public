# AI Discovery Webinar Rehearsal

**Date:** 29 April 2026  
**Location:** Microsoft Teams  
**Attendees:** Ciara Griffin, Jack Cunningham, Conor Flynn

## Summary

The team rehearsed the AI discovery webinar and reviewed the session flow,
slides, messaging and demo introduction. Feedback focused on making the
webinar educational, reducing slide density, improving visuals, and explaining
responsible AI adoption in a GXP setting.

## Discussion Points

- The webinar flow is welcome, AI discovery workshop explanation, complaints
  triage demo, then wrap-up.
- The core message is that AI work should begin with a defined business
  problem.
- The wording should refer to responsible adoption of AI in a GXP setting.
- Slides are currently too text-heavy and need more people-focused workshop
  visuals.
- The demo needs context before the screen is shown.
- The session should feel educational rather than sales-led.

## Decisions

- Use responsible adoption of AI in a GXP setting as the clearer wording.
- Keep the webinar tone educational rather than sales-focused.
